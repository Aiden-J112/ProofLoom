# Position the product as an AI learning workspace

The open-source product will be positioned as a local-first, evidence-grounded AI learning workspace rather than as a single autonomous Agent. Knowledge construction, exploration, and learning form the stable product surface, while question-answering, question-generation, and scoring Agents remain composable capabilities inside it; the first-version user is an owner-learner who imports, reviews, and learns from their own trusted material without a multi-role administration model.
